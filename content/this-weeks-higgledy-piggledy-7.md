Random thoughts
---------------

Experts are the anti-society. They make us think we don't know anything, we don't feel anything, that whatever we think is not informed or worse, not legitimate. They prevent us from taking part in society. They prevent us from even thinking we *could* take part in society. They make us spectators, and make it so that we never question that.
