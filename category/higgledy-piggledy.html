Hey! I'm Sébastien. Welcome to my homepage.

I'm a postdoc researcher interested in communication, interaction and culture — I currently work on deep learning methods for linguistic and network-structured data at IXXI in Lyon, France.

My PhD focused on real-life and experimental cases of chinese whispers, from a cognitive linguistics, anthropological and computational point of view. I was based at the EHESS in Paris and the Centre Marc Bloch in Berlin. If you want to know more, try looking at my research.

Below you'll find my blog, interested in building a society of commons, in education, and in humane technology that empowers and doesn't enslave. Also in learning, and opening black boxes.
